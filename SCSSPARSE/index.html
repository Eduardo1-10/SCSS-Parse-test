Gameplay

The goal of the game is to cleverly coerce and outwit all other players by strategically playing cards from your hand to the table in aiding your set collecting of illicit businesses a.k.a. ‘‘Rackets“. Win by being the first player to achieve four unique full sets of racket cards. Both luck and strategy are factors in winning the game. It can even get a little ruthless, but remember:

Expand Your Turf, Build Your Stash, and Run Rackets.

How To Win

Be the first player to collect and arrange four full sets of unique racket cards.

The Setup

How To Play

  • Each player starts with 6 cards in hand and picks up 2 cards each turn. 8 cards max.

  • Grow your turf by collecting racket cards.

    You can get racket cards by drawing them from the pickup or by using action cards to steal them from your opponents!

  • Protect your turf!

    You can use protection cards to keep your hard-earned rackets safe from scheming opponents. There are also action cards that allow you to “just say no.”

  • Demand cash from the competition.

    Use Pay Tribute cards to force payment from rivals. If they don’t have enough cash in their stash, they have to hand over their rackets.

The Buy Out

Need some extra muscle? The Buy Out move allows you to take a card from your stash back to your hand and then can be played in subsequent turns. Use this move wisely though… you can only do it once per game.
